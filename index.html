About Us

At Urban Home Design & Build we pride ourselves On providing our clients a quality, hands on and professional renovation service, specialising in kitchen, bathroom, complete home renovations and extensions.

Our team consists of a Registered and accredited Builder, in house interior designer As well as a team of certified trades.

With our team, you will have peace of mind that your Renovation project will be looked after from concept to completion. What sets us apart is that we work personally and directly with all of our clients From; design consultation and planning, hands on build/trades, project management, to ensure your renovation runs smoothly and every detail is considered.

We create beautiful spaces that are not only functional, but look and feel great also. If you are thinking about or planning for a kitchen, bathroom or home renovation feel free to get in touch to discuss your project and arrange for a free quote.
